FAQ
How long does a residential construction service take

On average, a typical residential construction takes 7 to 12 months in the US. However, it can largely vary depending on state laws and project requirements.

Will I be required to be present on the site during construction or remodeling?

You don’t necessarily have to be present on the site; we will communicate directly with you and update you at regular intervals about the timeline.

Is Houseura an expert at restoring a house from fire or storm damage?

Yes, at Houseura, our engineers, planners, and laborers are experts in providing complete restoration due to fire or storm.

What materials do you use as part of residential construction and remodeling?

We strongly work on the basis of the client’s requirements and satisfaction, ensuring that the material being used aligns with the client’s vision. Moreover, it is our responsibility that each building material involved in construction, from concrete to wooden panels, is made of the highest quality, reflecting the standards of our residential construction service.

Can I schedule a free estimate quotation for my residential construction with Houseura?

Yes, we are obliged to assist you with a free estimate quotation for your housing project. Simply fill out the query form on our website or contact us at info@houseura.com.
